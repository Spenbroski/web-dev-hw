My Reveiw

Shel Silverstein was an American Poet, Cartoonist, and Musician. He was the author and publisher of several children's books, including The Giving Tree, Where the Sidewalk Ends, and A Light in the Attic. Shel's poetry is matched with his unique art style and makes for a fantastic collection of poems and short stories.


"..This book will grab even the most reluctant reader's attention. It's great for introducing poetry to the younger students, and for including in poetry units for the older ones. The poems are a perfect way to provide some welcome comic relief after a stressful day in the classroom - for you and for them!"

- Bugs & Bunnies
