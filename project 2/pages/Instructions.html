Chocolate Mint Brownies

Instructions
Step #1 Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in syrup and vanilla. Add flour and salt; mix well.
Step #2 Pour into a greased 13x9 - in. baking pan. Bake 30 minutes (top of brownies will still appear wet). Cool on a wire rack.
Step #3 For filling, in a small bowl (A round, deep dish or basin used for food or liquid), cream butter and confectioners' sugar; add water, extract and food coloring until blended. Spread over cooled brownies. Refrigerate until set.
Step #4 For topping, melt chocolate chips and butter. Cool 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Spread over filling. Chill. Cut into bars. Store in refrigerator.
